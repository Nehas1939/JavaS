Coffee Lockdown

Three levels of roasting

Different roasting levels give a unique mouthfeel and experience. Go lighter for bright acidity and delicate flavors, medium for balanced sweetness and acidity, or darker for a richer, sharper taste.

Blends vs Single Origin

A blend is a mix of beans from various sources expertly combined for maximum balance and consistency. A single-origin coffee comes from a single place. Often in limited supply, single origin beans are loved for their distinctive flavors and taste. We offer both.
